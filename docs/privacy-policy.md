# Alora Privacy Policy (v1.0)

**Last Updated:** December 11, 2025

## 1. Data Collection
We collect:
- **Profile Data**: Name, Age, Gender, Photos.
- **Biometrics**: Face data for verification (processed locally/hashed).
- **Usage**: Swipes, Matches, Chat interactions for AI improvement.

## 2. AI Processing
- **Compatibility**: Your data is analyzed by our Deep Chemistry Engine.
- **Privacy**: We do not sell your raw data used for AI training to third parties.

## 3. Data Retention
- Active accounts: Indefinite unless requested.
- Deleted accounts: Hard delete after 30 days (soft delete immediately).

## 4. User Rights
- Right to Access (Download Data).
- Right to be Forgotten (Delete Account).
