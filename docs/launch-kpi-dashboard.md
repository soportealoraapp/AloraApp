# Launch KPI Dashboard

## 🎯 North Star Metric
- **Meaningful Connections**: Chats > 10 messages exchange.

## 📊 Core Metrics
### Growth
- **DAU/MAU**: Daily/Monthly Active Users.
- **K-Factor**: Viral coefficient (Referrals).

### Retention
- **D1**: > 40%
- **D7**: > 20%
- **D30**: > 10%

### Engagement
- **Matches/Day**: Avg matches per user.
- **Session Time**: Avg daily time in app.

### Quality
- **Crash Free Users**: > 99%.
- **Report Rate**: % of users reported (Safety).
